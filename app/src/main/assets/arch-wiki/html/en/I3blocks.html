i3blocks

Related articles

  • i3

i3blocks is an alternative status bar for the tiling wm i3. It's designed to replace the standard i3 status bar, i3status.

Installation

The following AUR packages provide i3blocks:

  • i3blocksAUR
  • i3blocks-gitAUR

Alternatively you can build it from source, with instructions available on the i3blocks github.
