Partition layout

/dev/sda1 and /dev/sdb1 10 GB -> /dev/md1; contains /

/dev/sda2 and /dev/sdb2 1 GB -> /dev/md2; swap

/dev/sda3 and /dev/sdb3 rest of space -> /dev/md3; /home
