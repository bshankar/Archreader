Owx

Notes: Too little content to warrant a separate page (Discuss in Talk:Owx#)

owx (open wouxun) is a CLI utility used to program KG669V radios by means of a CSV spreadsheet.

Installation

Install the owxAUR package.

Tips and Tricks
